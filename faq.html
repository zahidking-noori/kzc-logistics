Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions our clients ask.

What areas do you serve?
KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC serves Alexandria, VA, the greater DMV area, and international clients worldwide.
How can I request a quote?
You can request a quote by visiting our Contact page and filling out the form, or by calling us directly at (832) 292-7088.
Do you offer skilled and unskilled labor?
Yes, we provide both skilled and unskilled labor for various projects, short-term or long-term.
What is your return/refund policy?
Returns and refunds can be requested within 7 days of service delivery, depending on the service type. Please contact our support team for assistance.
How do you handle international shipping?
We provide customized international shipping and logistics solutions, ensuring safe and timely delivery of goods.
