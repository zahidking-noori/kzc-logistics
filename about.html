About Us — KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC

Excellence in Logistics, Maintenance, and Workforce Solutions

At KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC, we believe great service goes beyond delivery — it’s about reliability, professionalism, and trust. From our home base in Alexandria, Virginia, we provide logistics, property maintenance, labor supply, and global shipping services to clients across the United States and around the world.

Our Story

KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC was founded by Engineer Abdul Kabir Dawoodzai, an experienced logistics and maintenance professional originally from Afghanistan.

The company began as Kabir and Zak Logistics Services Co., a service provider supporting reconstruction and development projects throughout Afghanistan. Through its success and strong reputation, KZC evolved into a U.S.-based company focused on offering comprehensive logistics and general service solutions both locally and internationally.

Today, KZC stands as a symbol of reliability, efficiency, and global reach — combining American business standards with international expertise.

What We Do

  • Delivery & Logistics: Local and global transport of goods and packages with safety and precision.
  • Maintenance & Repairs: Professional upkeep for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.
  • Labor Supply: Reliable, skilled, and general laborers available for short or long-term projects.
  • Waste Removal: Responsible, eco-friendly waste management and site cleanup.
  • Custom Business Solutions: Tailored service packages to fit unique business needs.
