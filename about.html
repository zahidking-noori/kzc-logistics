About Us

Learn more about KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC and our commitment to excellence.

Our Story

KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC is a trusted provider of comprehensive logistics, maintenance, labor supply, and waste removal services. Based in Alexandria, Virginia, we serve businesses and individuals across the United States and beyond.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions tailored to each client's unique needs. Whether you need local delivery, skilled labor for a construction project, or regular property maintenance, KZC has you covered.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to be your go-to partner for all your logistics and support needs.

Our History & Growth

Our Journey. Our Growth.

KZC Logistics & Solutions LLC began with a simple idea — to help businesses move smarter, operate cleaner, and grow faster. Starting with small delivery services and labor support, we expanded into maintenance, property support, fleet assistance, and nationwide logistics.

Over the years, our team has supported construction companies, government contractors, commercial facilities, and private clients across multiple states. Each year, we continue adding services, resources, and trained personnel to match the growing needs of our clients.

Today, KZC stands as a multi-service partner built on professionalism, flexibility, and consistent performance — positioning us for continued growth in the U.S. and beyond.
